Crispy Roasted Chickpeas

Image and recipe from The Mediterranean Dish.

Description

These chickpeas are a great way to add flavor, protein, and a crunch to salads. Choose seasonings that complement your dish. I often replace the salt with soy sauce before roasting.

Ingredients

  • Cooked chickpeas
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Your choice of seasoning: basil, oregano, rosemary, Old Bay, za'atar, thyme, red pepper, paprika, cumin, coriandar...

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Use a colandar to dry the chickpeas.
  3. In a bowl, coat the chickpease with olive oil and sprinkle salt throughout.
  4. Spread the chickpeas into a single layer on a baking sheet.
  5. Roast for 20 to 35 minutes, or until the chickpease turn golden brown and crispy to your liking. Shake the sheet pan every 10 minutes or so.
  6. Season the chickpeas immediately after roasting. Season chickpeas after they come out of the oven, not before!
