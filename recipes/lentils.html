Steven's Lentils

Image from The Endless Meal.

Description

A creamy, tomato- and coconut-milk based lentil curry, delicious over rice or millet. Easy to make. A Sherwood classic!

Ingredients

  • 2 tbs. coconut oil
  • 1 tbs. cumin seed
  • 1 tbs. coriandar seed
  • 10ish cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbs. ginger, minced
  • 1 tbs. turmeric
  • 2 tsp. salt
  • 1 large can crushed tomato
  • 1.5 c. green lentils
  • 3 c. water
  • 1 small can coconut milk
  • cilantro, chopped, for garnish

Steps

  1. In a dutch oven or similarly sized pot, heat the coconut oil. Then add the cumin and corindar seed and heat for about 30 seconds.
  2. Add the garlic, and heat for another 30 seconds or so.
  3. Add the tomatoes, turmeric, ginger, and salt. Let simmer for a few minutes.
  4. Add the lentils and water. Simmer until lentils are fully cooked -- 30-40 minutes. If they start to dry out, add a little more water.
  5. Add the coconut milk, stir, and heat for a few more minutes.
  6. Serve over rice or millet, garnished with the cilanto.
